Best Time to Visit Manila: A Quick Guide

Dry Season

Dec - May: Ideal for outdoor activities, city walks, and local festivals.

Rainy Season

Jun - Nov: Frequent rain and typhoons. Great for indoor activities like museums.

Best Festival Time

Jan, Feb, Jun, Dec: Experience Manila's vibrant cultural festivals!

Packing & Travel Tips

Dry Season (Dec – May)

  • Pack lightweight clothes and sunscreen.
  • Comfortable walking shoes for sightseeing.
  • Don’t forget a hat or sunglasses for sunny days.

Rainy Season (Jun – Nov)

  • Carry an umbrella and a raincoat.
  • Wear waterproof shoes or sandals.
  • Bring a waterproof phone case for your electronics.

Upcoming Festivals & Events

Annual Events in Manila

  • January: Feast of the Black Nazarene (Quiapo)
  • February: Chinese New Year in Binondo
  • June: Independence Day Celebration at Rizal Park
  • December: Christmas Lights and Parols Festival

🌞 Dry Season (Dec – May)

This is the best time to walk around the city comfortably, visit historical spots, and join local events without worrying about rain.

🌧️ Rainy Season (Jun – Nov)

Frequent rains and potential typhoons. Best to plan indoor visits like museums and galleries. Bring an umbrella!
